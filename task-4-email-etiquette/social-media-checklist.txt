✅ Do’s
* ✔️ Think before you post—consider how it may affect your reputation
* ✔️ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
* ✔️ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
* ✔️ Maintain privacy settings and control who can see your posts
* ✔️ Be respectful and polite in online interactions


❌ Don’ts
* ❌ Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
* ❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior
* ❌ Don’t accept friend requests from unknown people
* ❌ Don’t click on suspicious links or ads
* ❌ Don’t overshare details about your daily routine or location
