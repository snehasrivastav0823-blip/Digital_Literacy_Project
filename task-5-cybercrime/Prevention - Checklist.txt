✅ General Safety Tips
* ✔️ Create strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, and symbols) and avoid reusing them
* ✔️ Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for email, social media, and important apps
* ✔️ Don’t click on unknown links or download files from untrusted sources
* ✔️ Check website URLs carefully (look for HTTPS and correct spelling) before entering details
* ✔️ Log out from accounts when using shared or public computers
* ✔️ Be cautious about what you post—avoid sharing personal details like phone number, address, or college ID


* 💰 UPI / Financial Safety Tips
* ✔️ Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from customer care
* ✔️ Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID/name before sending money
* ✔️ Do not accept unknown “collect” or “request money” notifications on UPI apps
* ✔️ Use trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm and keep them updated


🚨 Report Cybercrime Immediately
* 🌐 Report online fraud at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* 📞 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (especially for financial fraud cases)
