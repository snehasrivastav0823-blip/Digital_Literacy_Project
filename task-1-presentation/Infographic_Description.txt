This infographic titled “Digital Literacy Essentials” presents key concepts needed for students to navigate the digital world safely and effectively. It explains digital literacy as the ability to use digital tools responsibly, highlighting skills like accessing information, evaluating content, and communicating online. The infographic also focuses on safe internet practices, such as using strong passwords, checking for secure (HTTPS) websites, avoiding phishing scams, and being cautious on public Wi-Fi networks.
In addition, it emphasizes the importance of building a professional online presence, encouraging students to create profiles, showcase their skills, and keep their information updated on platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub. Finally, it introduces useful tools for students, including Canva and Google Scholar. Overall, the infographic uses clear sections and visuals to make digital literacy easy to understand and apply.
